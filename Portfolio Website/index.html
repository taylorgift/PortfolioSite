Taylor has been recording and mixing artists since 2009. He graduated from Texas State University in 2016 with a bachelor of science in sound recording technology. Taylor has spent several years recording clients at Fire Station Studios and Blue Rock Studios. This is also where he studied under renowned engineers such as Charlie Kramsky, Gary Hickinbotham, Chet Himes, and Keith Gary.

Texas State is where Taylor discovered his passion for technology. Along his degree path he picked up a minor in computer science and has been developing audio software and websites like the one you are viewing now!

Currently, Taylor is working with Phat Sounds Media, a startup media company focusing on providing quality audio/video recordings as well as live streaming events.
